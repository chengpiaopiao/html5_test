localStorage.removeItem()

Storage 接口的 removeItem() 方法，接受一个键名作为参数，会把该键名从存储中移除。

            localStorage.setItem('key1', 'value1');
            localStorage.getItem('key1');

            localStorage.removeItem('key1');
            localStorage.getItem('key1');
